You May Not Be Eligible for an ITIN

ITINs are issued only to non-US persons who cannot obtain a Social Security Number. US citizens and Green Card holders must use their SSN for all US federal tax filings.

An SSN Already Covers Your Tax Filing Needs

If you have a Social Security Number, you do not need an ITIN — your SSN is used for all US federal tax purposes. The IRS will not issue an ITIN to someone who holds an SSN.
